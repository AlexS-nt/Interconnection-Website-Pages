Why do people Visit the Great Barrier Reef

The Great barrier reef is the worlds largest Coral Reef System, with a large biodiversity, People visit the reef for many reasons including:

  • Large biodiversity: The Great Barrier Reef has a large biodiversity, Containing Giantclambs, Rare species of whales, six out of seven of the worlds marine species amd over 1,600 species of fish
  • Snorkeling and Diving: The clear waters in the great barrier reef give excelent opitinitues for snorking and diving, meaning the vast array of biodiversity can be expirenced up close
  • Natural Wonders: The great barrier reef, while on it's own is a breath taking expericence, the nearby scenery also is stunning,
